## Relationship to StyxCD and Kharon

Johnny-Johnny is part of a broader engineering ecosystem.

### StyxCD

StyxCD is a deterministic software delivery platform focused on workflow orchestration, application deployment, and operational visibility.

It executes well-defined engineering workflows where deterministic behavior is preferred over AI-driven decision making.

### Kharon

Kharon is an AI engineering assistant built specifically for StyxCD.

Its purpose is to help users understand and operate StyxCD by providing context-aware assistance, documentation, troubleshooting, planning, and operational insight within the platform.

Kharon is a product feature of the StyxCD ecosystem rather than a general-purpose assistant.

### Johnny-Johnny

Johnny-Johnny is a broader personal engineering assistant.

Rather than focusing on a single product, it provides reusable capabilities that assist with everyday engineering work, including:

- Project management
- Backlog synchronization
- Documentation generation
- GitHub automation
- Email and calendar assistance
- Knowledge management
- Planning and reasoning
- Engineering workflow automation

Johnny-Johnny may interact with StyxCD, GitHub, cloud providers, productivity tools, and other systems, but it is not limited to any one platform.

Together, the three projects occupy different layers:

```text
Johnny-Johnny
    Personal Engineering Assistant
              │
              ▼
          Kharon
     StyxCD AI Assistant
              │
              ▼
          StyxCD
Deterministic Delivery Platform
```
